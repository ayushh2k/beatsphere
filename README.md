# Beatsphere

### [➡️ Explore the Map Live at beatsphere.live ⬅️](https://beatsphere.live)



---

BeatSphere is a social music discovery platform that visualizes what people are listening to on a real-time, interactive map.

## Core Features

-   **Live Map Visualization:** See users and their currently playing music in your vicinity.
-   **Music Taste Matching:** Discover people who share your favorite artists and genres through Last.fm integration.
-   **Real-time Chat:** Instantly connect and chat with other users on the map.
-   **Cross-Platform:** Available on both Web and the Google Play Store.
